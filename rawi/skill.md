### Blog Post Structure

```
# Title (60 chars, keyword-rich)

Meta description (155 chars)

## Introduction
- Hook the reader
- State the problem
- Promise the solution

## Main Sections (H2s)
- Use subheadings (H3s)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Include images/examples

## Conclusion
- Summarize key points
- Clear CTA

---
Word count: 1,500-2,500 for SEO
Keywords: Primary (3-5x), Secondary (1-2x each)
```

### Email Sequence

```
Email 1: Welcome
- Thank them for signing up
- Set expectations
- One simple CTA

Email 2: Value (Day 2)
- Teach something useful
- Show product benefit
- Soft CTA

Email 3: Social Proof (Day 4)
- Customer story/testimonial
- Results and outcomes
- Medium CTA

Email 4: Overcome Objections (Day 6)
- Address common concerns
- FAQ style
- Strong CTA

Email 5: Urgency (Day 7)
- Limited time/offer
- Final push
- Clear CTA
```

## Questions to Ask Before Creating Content

1. What platform is this for?
2. What is the goal (awareness, engagement, conversion)?
3. Who is the target audience?
4. Arabic, English, or both?
5. What is the key message?
6. Is there a specific CTA?
7. Any brand guidelines to follow?
8. What tone - professional, casual, funny?
